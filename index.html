Traditional Wear

Shalwar Kameez

The staple of Pakistani fashion, perfect for casual and formal occasions with intricate embroidery and vibrant colors.

Anarkali Dresses

Inspired by the Mughal era, these dresses are ideal for weddings and special events, exuding elegance and grace.

Gharara design

Wide-legged pants paired with short or long kurtas, adorned with intricate embroidery, zari work, and mirror work.

Contemporary Styles

Western Fusion

A unique blend of traditional elements with modern western styles, creating trendy and stylish outfits.

Casual Wear

Comfortable and stylish options for everyday wear, from trendy t-shirts and jeans to chic tunics and leggings.

Party Wear

Stylish dresses, jumpsuits, and skirts featuring sequins, beads, and lace, perfect for any event.

Seasonal Trends

Summer Collections

Lightweight fabrics like lawn, cotton, and chiffon with floral prints and pastel colors, ideal for the hot summer months.

Winter Collections

Cozy fabrics like velvet, wool, and khaddar, with warm shawls, jackets, and sweaters for the colder months.

Accessories

Jewelry

From traditional jhumkas and bangles to modern necklaces and bracelets, something to complement every outfit.

Footwear

Traditional khussas and sandals, along with trendy sneakers and ballet flats for a contemporary look.

Dupattas and Scarves

Versatile accessories that add color and style to any outfit, available in various fabrics, colors, and designs.
